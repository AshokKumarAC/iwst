    INTERNSHIP OPPORTUNITIES FOR ENGINEERING STUDENTS

    The students pursuing Pre-Final & Final Year of Graduate & Post Graduate engineering in Mechanical, Electrical, Electronics, Computer Science and few of the allied disciplines are eligible to apply for these internships/ Project Work initiated by Ministry of Heavy Industries, Government of India, provided students have the required academic background and passion to work on the problems posed by the industries.

    Facilities

    Also candidates who have completed Graduation / Post Graduation in Engineering in the above branches are eligible to apply to work on live projects.
