    Advanced Woodworking Training Center

    The Advanced Woodworking Training Center (AWTC) is a premier training centre is located in the heart of Bangalore city. It was established by the Institute of Wood Science & Technology (IWST) as the first Training Center in India with an aim to enhance skillset of manpower working in wood-based industries. In this endeavour, AWTC provides trained manpower to the wood based industries for handling advanced machines and tools to work on various wood and wood products that meet the global standards.

    Over the last one and half decade, AWTC trained to more than 2500 professionals who are proficient in handing state of the art machines used in the wood working. It houses more than 40 different categories of advanced machines and tools to impart the skills to the trainees. The short term training courses and diploma offered by the AWTC is taught by highly experienced and well trained master trainers. Considering the research and training facilities available IWST and AWTC respectively, Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship, Government of India has recognized as a Centre of Excellence in the field of skilling ecosystem in the country.

    AWTC was established in 2003 as a joint venture between Government of India (through Institute of Wood Science & Technology) and Italian Government (through Italian Trade Commission and Italian Woodworking Machines &Tools Manufacturers Association) and it was run jointly till 2011. Presently, it is being managed by the IWST which is a premier research institute works under the aegis of Indian Council of Forestry Research & Education (ICFRE), which is an autonomous body of Ministry of Environment & Forests, Government of India.

    About the Course

    VISION

    Course is biased on actual transfer of skills through hands on experience simulating the factory conditions.

    MISSION

    We are passionate about creating opportunities for creative learning, to provide students with professional, and practical learning experiences, so that they become competent, valuable professionals, and to maximize their career opportunities in wood based industry.

    MANDATE

    To provide trained manpower to wood based industries. To up skill the wood working professionals using latest and state of the art machineries prevalent in the market. To co-ordinate among researchers, wood working professionals and wood based industries for over all development of wood & furniture sector.

    Diploma in Advanced Woodworking (1 Year)

    Description: The Diploma Course was launched in the year 2018-19 jointly with M/s. Biesse Manufacturing Company Private Limited. This program offers an excellent opportunity for trainees to acquire required skill set to work on wood and wood products. This course provides an opportunity to the students to earn while they are learing skills through industry attachment for a period of six months. This course has eight major modules namely, Fundamentals of wood materials, Fundamentals of Engineering, Wood processing using advanced machines& allied processes, Loading & unloading systems, machinery safety, maintenance of machines, Assembly & Joinery, Advanced application of software (CNC, CAD/CAM & 3D-Pytha) and project work. Upon successful completion of training, the trainees will be able to handle most of the advanced woodworking machines that used in the wood based industry.

    Course Benefits:

    •   Earn while learn program provides opportunity of industrial attachment to the candidates for a period of six months

    •   Acquire complete knowledge on woodworking

    •   Complete knowledge on manufacturing of Solid Wood & Panel Products

    •   Exposure to Industry practices on woodworking process

    •   To have complete Knowledge on 2D and 3D programming in CNC Machine

    •   Complete Knowledge on executing the Panel processing on CNC

    •   Assured placement support in India & Overseas.

    Eligibility: Pass in Pre-University Course/Senior Secondary/XII/Equivalent from recognized Board. (Graduates in Science/Forestry/Engineering can also apply).

    Course Fee: Rs.35,000/-for the entire course; Extra:Rs.1,650/- per month towards Accommodation Charges Food Charges (as per actual)
