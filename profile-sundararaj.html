    Profile

    Research Experience:

    Areas of Specialization:

    Entomology

    Education

    M.Sc (Zoology), M.Phil., Ph.D

    Research Activities

    Research Interests:
    Insect biodiversity

    Whitefly taxonomy

    Wood protection

    Insect Pest Management

    Research Expertise

    Whitefly biosystematics and Development of pest management practices.

    Projects

    Utilization of neem constituents for the management of forest insect pests of arid and semi-arid regions.

    Studies on the whiteflies (Aleyrodidae: Homoptera) of arid and semi-arid region of India.

    Studies on termite problems on trees and timber and development of termite testing facilities.

    Investigations on the potential of Medicinal and Aromatic plants as a source of potential insecticides.

    Studies on the diversity of Aleyrodids (Aleyrodidae: Homoptera) in South Western Ghats.

    Current Research Projects:

    Bioecology, Damage Potential And Management Of Gall Formers Of Pongamia Pinnata (L.) Pierre
