    Profile

    Research Experience:
    Research experience of 23 years (Since 1992) in wood science and technology at Institute of Wood Science & Technology, Bangalore (India).

    Research experience of 4 years (from 1988 to 1992) at National Physical Laboratory, Delhi in the field of material science.

    Areas of Specialization:

    Wood Science,
    Wood Technology,
    Material Science,
    Low temperature physics

    Education

    Ph.D. (Physics), Indian Institute of Technology, New Delhi
    M.C.A., Indira Ghandhi National Open University, New Delhi
    M.Sc. (Physics), Lucknow University, Lucknow (UP)

    Research Activities

    Research Interests:
    Wood Quality
    Wood Physics
    Wood Mechanics
    Wood Modification
    Nano-metal Based Wood Preservatives
    Non-destructive Testing and Evaluation (NDT&E)
    Layered Wood Composites

    Research Expertise

    Assessment of wood quality of plantation timbers including clones and hybrids
    Anatomical, physical and mechanical properties
    Thermal and chemical modification of wood
    Fracture mechanics of wood and its composites
    Nano-technology for improving wood products
    Evaluation of properties by NDT techniques (NIR, ultrasonic etc.)
    Surface profiling and characterization
    Load bearing reconstituted wood composites


    Projects

    Production of reconstituted wood products/wood composites - laminated veneer lumber (LVL) from different plantation grown timber species and to study their different physical and mechanical properties. ICFRE funded project.

    Studies on influence of microfibril angle and spiral grains in wood on the strength properties of plantation grown timbers (Eucalyptus spp.). ICFRE funded project.

    Study of Anatomical, physical and mechanical properties of plantation grown Acacia auriculaeformis, Acacia mangium, Tecomella undulata and Eucalyptus tereticornis clones. ICFRE funded project.

    Relationship of the wood properties of coppice plantation grown Eucalyptus species. ICFRE funded project.

    Evaluation of physical and mechanical properties of Acacia auriculaeformis x A.mangium hybrid, A. mangium x Acacia auriculaeformis hybrid, Acacia auriculaeformis (spring vale provenance and local) and A. mangium (local). Sponsored M/s Mysore Papermills Ltd., Bhadravathi (Karnataka).

    Assessment of wood quality (Anatomical) of 8-10�year-old Acacia auriculaeformis and Acacia mangium hybrid. ICFRE funded project.

    Evaluation of wood quality parameters of plantation grown Eucalyptus citriodora Hook for different end uses. ICFRE funded project.

    Assessment of wood quality of Tectona grandis (teak) clones from Thithimathi (Karnataka) and Andhra Pradesh. ICFRE funded project.

    Studies on productivity and management of Teak (Tectona grandis) in agroforestry practices in Karnataka and Andhra Pradesh. ICFRE funded project.

    Studies on fracture mechanics in solid wood and wood composites using acoustic emissions. ICFRE funded project.

    Utilization of lesser used low-density wood species for value-added products and performance evaluation�. Laboratory of Wood Science and Technology, Department of Forestry, Michigan State University, East Lansing, Michigan, USA.

    Detection of natural and biological defects in timbers by non-destructive testing techniques. ICFRE funded project.

    Studies on acoustical behaviour of plantation timbers for musical instruments and wall paneling. Council of Scientific and Industrial Research, New Delhi funded project.

    Utilization of Bambusa bambos (L.) and Dendrocalamus strictus (Roxb.) as an alternative of wooden dunnage pallets. ICFRE funded project.

    Comparative study of clones of Eucalypts and Acacia hybrid for handicraft sector. ICFRE funded project.

    Enhancing dimensional stability and durability of wood for flooring application by eco-friendly thermal processing. ICFRE funded project.

    Wood quality of plantation teak grown in Andaman and Nicobar Island"- Project funded by the Department of Environment and Forests, Andaman & Nicobar Admin., Port Blair.

    Strength properties of finger joints of A. auriculaeformis A. Cunn. ex Benth of different ages (6-7 and 10-11years old) for various end uses. ICFRE funded project.

    Furfurylation of wood to improve properties for various applications. ICFRE funded project.

    Effect of treatment with micronized copper preservative on the strength, treatability and durability of selected wood species. ICFRE funded project.

    Evaluation and comparison of wood quality of teakwood produced from tissue culture and seed sources. Funded by M/s Shivashakti Biotechnologies Ltd., Hyderabad.

    Achievements

    Thermal modification of wood for value addition to plantation timbers. (Sponsored by Department of Forests and Wildlife Preservation, Government of Punjab, Punjab)

    Evaluation of wood Properties of Melia composita of different ages from southern India for finding suitability for various uses and development of value added products. (ICFRE Funding).
