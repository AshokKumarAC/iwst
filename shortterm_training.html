    About the Course

    Short Term Training Course

    Course is biased on actual transfer of skills through hands on experience simulating the factory conditions.

    OBJECTIVE

    Training in veneer peeling, resin manufacture, etc and standardization as well as specific training in the mode of transfer of technologies

    AIMS

    Upgrade the skill in their specialized field of interest

    Short Term Training

    IWST undertakes and organizes training programmes in different disciplines for different target groups ranging from the level of technicians to managers on sponsorship basis and also for national and international students. By organizing several short term training courses, the institute is continuing to draw attention of several small and medium scale enterprises.

    Various Short term vocational training courses have been conducted for technical personnel from industry to upgrade their skill in the specialized field of interest such as veneer peeling, resin manufacture, panel/sheet manufacture, testing and standardization as well as specific training in the mode of transfer of technologies. A few courses have also been conducted to train artisans/rural people engaged in bamboo related activities for mechanized slivering of bamboo required for mat making, NGOs, engineers and architects in bamboo based housing and entrepreneurs for different bamboo based technologies.

    Short term courses are also conducted at IWST Field Station, Kolkata on topics related to manufacturing and testing of wood and wood based panel material.

    Short term courses for 1-5 days on the following disciplines

    • Plywood Manufacturing Technology-I (Veneer processing, log storage, centering, peeling, clipping, drying and knife grinding)

    • Plywood Manufacturing Technology-II (Economic adhesive, resin manufacture, gluing, hot pressing)

    • Veneer peeling techniques

    • Peeling and knife grinding

    • Knife grinding practices for peeler and slicer knives

    • Flitching of logs, veneer slicing, matching and knife grinding

    • Flitching of logs and veneer slicing

    • Resin manufacturing, pre-pressing and hot pressing

    • Block board and Flush Door Manufacturing

    • Production management in plywood industry

    • Trouble shooting in plywood manufacture

    • Testing of panel products

    • Sawmilling, seasoning, finger-jointing, & glulam techniques

    • Sawmilling and seasoning

    • Wood seasoning

    • Good practices in carpentry & wood finishing techniques

    • Maintenance of Band saws and TCT saws

    • Bamboo primary processing

    • Bamboo primary processing and Mat making

    • Preservative treatment for Bamboo

    • Processing of Bamboo Mat Board

    • Bamboo based housing system
